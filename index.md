# Running knowledge base

An evidence-graded knowledge base on distance running, biased toward what is real, science-based and proven, and honest about what is not. Start with [the basics](concepts/the-basics.md); it puts everything else in proportion.

Every claim-bearing page carries an `evidence:` grade (`strong` / `moderate` / `limited` / `weak` / `contested`) and cites its sources at the point of each claim. See the [contributing guide](CONTRIBUTING.md) for the schema and editorial stance.

## The training environment

How and with whom you train. [Run clubs and group running](techniques/run-clubs.md), and [parkrun](techniques/parkrun.md) in particular, are among the most reliable ways to stay consistent, though the headline health gains are mostly measured in self-selected joiners. [Athletics clubs](techniques/athletics-clubs.md) unlock affiliation, licensed coaching and the competition calendar, with no proven club effect beyond self-selection. [Coaching](techniques/coaching.md) helps most through individualisation and accountability, but no trial isolates a coaching effect over self-coaching. The [training apps](gear/training-apps.md) that track and prescribe give a modest, fading nudge, while their load metrics and AI plans are weaker than they look.

## Racing distances ([events/](events/index.md))

Each event as a unit: its energy-system demands, its dominant limiter and the training it rewards, spun off the [distance-specific training](techniques/distance-specific-training.md) overview. On the track, the [800 m](events/the-800m.md) (won on speed reserve and lactate tolerance), the [1500 m and the mile](events/the-1500m-and-mile.md) (VO₂max and a kick) and the [3000 m](events/the-3000m.md) (the bridge to long distance). On the road and longer track, the [5k](events/the-5k.md) (the classic VO₂max event), the [10k](events/the-10k.md) (VO₂max leaning to threshold), the [half marathon](events/the-half-marathon.md) (a threshold event) and the [marathon](events/the-marathon.md) (threshold, economy, durability and fuel). Ultras keep their own page under [ultramarathon and trail training](techniques/ultra-and-trail-training.md), and [cross country](events/cross-country.md) is the off-road winter discipline, decided by terrain rather than distance.

## Injury ([injury/](injury/index.md))

Staying on the road beats every marginal gain, so [injury prevention](injury/injury-prevention.md) and a sensible [return to running](techniques/return-to-running.md) earn their own pages. Most [running injuries](injury/running-injuries.md) are overuse from training-load errors: [bone stress injuries](injury/bone-stress-injuries.md), [tendinopathy](injury/tendinopathy.md) (including [Achilles](injury/achilles-tendinopathy.md)), [patellofemoral pain](injury/patellofemoral-pain.md), [plantar fasciopathy](injury/plantar-fasciopathy.md), [calf and hamstring strains](injury/muscle-strains.md), the remaining [common overuse injuries](injury/common-overuse-injuries.md) (iliotibial band, shin splints), and the everyday [practical niggles](injury/practical-niggles.md) and [muscle cramps](injury/muscle-cramps.md). The hands-on treatments people reach for are graded honestly in [manual and passive therapies](injury/manual-and-passive-therapies.md): [shockwave](injury/shockwave-therapy.md) is the one standout, while [chiropractic](injury/chiropractic.md), [osteopathy](injury/osteopathy.md), [dry needling](injury/dry-needling.md), [acupuncture](injury/acupuncture.md) and the [soft-tissue therapies](injury/soft-tissue-therapies.md) (ART, Graston, cupping) mostly offer short-term, non-specific relief. General knowledge, not medical advice.
