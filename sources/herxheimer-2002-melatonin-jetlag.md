# Herxheimer & Petrie 2002, Melatonin for jet lag (Cochrane)

Cochrane systematic review. Melatonin taken close to the target bedtime at the destination is effective at reducing jet lag from crossing five or more time zones, and more so for eastward travel. Doses of 0.5-5 mg work similarly, with higher doses more sedating. Cochrane review. Moderate.
