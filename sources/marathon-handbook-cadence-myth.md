# Marathon Handbook, the 180-cadence myth

Coaching summary of cadence evidence: 180 steps per minute is a guideline drawn from elites at race pace, not a universal target; cadence rises with pace; optimal cadence is individual and rises with height and pace. A secondary, practitioner-facing synthesis rather than primary research.
