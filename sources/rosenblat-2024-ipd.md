# Rosenblat et al. 2025, Polarised vs pyramidal individual-participant meta-analysis

No overall difference between polarised and pyramidal distributions; the paper reports a training-level interaction: competitive athletes may benefit more from polarised, recreational athletes more from pyramidal. Moderate-strong.
