# Fitzgerald 2014, 80/20 Running

Matt Fitzgerald's book popularising the 80/20 rule for amateurs: roughly 80% of training at low intensity and 20% hard, translating Stephen Seiler's research on elite intensity distribution into practical plans. The underlying easy-hard principle is well supported; the specific 80/20 prescription is a popularisation rather than original science. Moderate (for the underlying principle).
