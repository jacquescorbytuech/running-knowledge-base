# Anderson et al. 2020, changing foot strike pattern

Systematic review and meta-analysis of the benefits and risks of changing foot strike pattern, covering injury, running economy and biomechanics (53 studies). Found only limited, retrospective evidence linking non-rearfoot strike to lower injury rates, no clear economy advantage for either pattern, and called for prospective trials. A review of mixed-quality evidence.
