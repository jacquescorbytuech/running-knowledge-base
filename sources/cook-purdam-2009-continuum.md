# Cook & Purdam 2009, Continuum model of tendon pathology

Influential model describing tendinopathy as a load-driven continuum (reactive, dysrepair, degenerative) marked by cell and matrix change and collagen disorganisation, not classic inflammation. Reframes 'tendinitis' as 'tendinopathy'. Moderate to strong (widely adopted conceptual framework supported by histology).
