# Areta et al. 2023, critical review of the energy availability threshold

Critical review arguing that the evidence for a single ~30 kcal/kg fat-free mass/day threshold for menstrual disturbance is weaker than often presented. Responses vary between individuals and the relationship looks more like a graded dose-response than a sharp cut-off. Moderate; a reasoned critique rather than new experimental data.
