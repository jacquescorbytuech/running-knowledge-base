# Body composition and distance-running performance, narrative synthesis

Practitioner synthesis of body-composition and running data. Within a healthy range a higher power-to-weight ratio helps, but pushing body fat too low (below roughly 5% in men or 10–15% in women) harms immune function, bone and performance, so there is an optimal range rather than 'lower is better'. Limited; narrative practitioner source summarising primary studies.
