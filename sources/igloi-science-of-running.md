# Science of Running, on the Iglói interval method

Coaching analysis of Iglói's method: large volumes of short intervals (100-400 m) run by perceived effort, prescribed with terms such as "fresh" (~60%), "good" (~70%) and "hard", rather than fixed paces. Coaching interpretation, not peer-reviewed.
