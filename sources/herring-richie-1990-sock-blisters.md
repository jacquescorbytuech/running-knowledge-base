# Herring & Richie 1990, Sock fibre and friction blisters

Double-blind trial of 35 long-distance runners in socks identical except for fibre. Cotton produced roughly twice as many blisters, about three times larger, than acrylic. Small and dated, but a controlled signal that synthetic fibre beats cotton for blister prevention. Moderate.
