# England Athletics, Coach licensing and safeguarding

In the UK, holding an athletics coaching licence is conditional: it requires a valid enhanced DBS check and completion of the athletics-specific safeguarding module, and it must be renewed on a three-year cycle with an updated DBS check and a repeat safeguarding course. The licence verifies a safeguarding and vetting floor and a baseline qualification; it does not certify coaching quality or individualisation. Strong organisational fact.
