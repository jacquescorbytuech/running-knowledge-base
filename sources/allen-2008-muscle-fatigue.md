# Allen, Lamb & Westerblad 2008, cellular muscle fatigue

Authoritative review of peripheral fatigue mechanisms: accumulating inorganic phosphate and impaired calcium handling in the sarcoplasmic reticulum reduce force, with hydrogen ions a smaller contributor than once thought. The standard account of how muscle fatigues from within. A review in Physiological Reviews.
