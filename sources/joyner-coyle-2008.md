# Joyner & Coyle 2008, Endurance exercise performance

Review (J Physiol) establishing VO2max, lactate threshold and running economy as the integrated determinants of endurance performance. Reports that champion endurance athletes have VO2max values of 70–85 ml/kg/min, and that elite marathon runners sustain approximately 75–85% of VO2max during the race. Strong.
