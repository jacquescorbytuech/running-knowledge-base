# Tart cherry and recovery scoping review (Front Nutr 2026)

A scoping review of Montmorency tart cherry consumption for athletic performance and post-exercise recovery in healthy adults. It finds a moderate benefit for the recovery of muscle strength and power after strenuous exercise and a weaker, less consistent effect on muscle soreness, with performance effects more variable.

## Key takeaways

- Tart cherry taken before and after strenuous exercise has a moderate positive effect on recovery of muscle strength and power.
- The effect on subjective muscle soreness is smaller and less consistent.
- Direct performance benefits are more variable across the reviewed studies.
- The benefit is attributed to anthocyanin antioxidant and anti-inflammatory action, the same mechanism behind the adaptation-blunting concern for high-dose antioxidants.
