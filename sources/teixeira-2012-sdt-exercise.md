# Teixeira et al. 2012, Self-determination theory and exercise

Systematic review of 66 studies applying self-determination theory to exercise and physical activity. Autonomous motivation and satisfaction of the basic needs for autonomy, competence and relatedness consistently predict adherence. The relatedness component supports why a social setting can help people keep training, though most evidence is correlational and the group element is hard to isolate. Moderate.
