# Farrokhyar et al. 2015, vitamin D status in athletes meta-analysis

Systematic review and meta-analysis finding a high prevalence of vitamin D inadequacy in athletes, with greater risk at higher latitudes, in winter and in indoor sports. Moderate; pooled observational data with variable cut-offs.
