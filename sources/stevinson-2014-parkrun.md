# Stevinson & Hickson 2014, Public health potential of parkrun

Survey of 7308 parkrun participants across England. About a quarter reported doing little or no regular physical activity before taking part, evidence that the format reaches some previously inactive people. A self-selected cross-sectional sample, so it describes who signs up rather than proving a population effect. Moderate.
