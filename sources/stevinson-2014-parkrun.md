# Stevinson & Hickson 2014, Public health potential of parkrun

Survey of 354 new parkrun registrants at a single English event. About a quarter reported doing no regular running or jogging before registering, evidence that the format reaches some previously inactive people. A self-selected cross-sectional sample, so it describes who signs up rather than proving a population effect. Moderate.
