# Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics 2016, Position on vegetarian diets

Formal position of the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics: appropriately planned vegetarian and vegan diets are healthful, nutritionally adequate and appropriate for all life stages, including for athletes. The qualifier "appropriately planned" carries the weight: adequacy depends on meeting energy and key micronutrient needs. Strong (position statement of a major dietetic body).
