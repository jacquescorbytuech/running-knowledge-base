# Mountjoy et al. 2018, IOC RED-S update, screening and return to play

The 2018 IOC RED-S update, refining the model and adding the RED-S Clinical Assessment Tool and a risk-stratified return-to-play framework. Recommends screening at-risk athletes and managing low energy availability through a sports physician, dietitian and, where needed, mental-health support. Strong as a consensus statement.
