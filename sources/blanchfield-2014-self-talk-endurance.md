# Blanchfield et al. 2014, Self-talk and endurance performance

Pretest-posttest trial (n=24): a two-week motivational self-talk intervention reduced perceived exertion and increased cycling time to exhaustion by about 18%. A direct test of the psychobiological mechanism, showing that altering effort perception alters endurance. Moderate (single small study, cycling).
