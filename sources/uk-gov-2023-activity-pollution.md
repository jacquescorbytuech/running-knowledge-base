# UK Government 2025, Physical activity and exposure to air pollution

UK Government evidence review on exercising in polluted air. For most people the long-term benefits of regular physical activity outweigh the short-term risks of exercising during elevated-pollution episodes, and the recommended response is to reduce personal exposure (avoid busy roads and rush hour, train away from traffic) rather than to stop exercising. Moderate (government position, consistent with the literature).
