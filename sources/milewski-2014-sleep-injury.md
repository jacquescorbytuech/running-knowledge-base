# Milewski et al. 2014, Sleep and injury in adolescent athletes

Prospective study of adolescent athletes: those sleeping under 8 hours a night had about 1.7 times the injury risk of those who slept more. The most-cited single study linking short sleep to injury, though limited to adolescents. Limited to moderate (single observational cohort).
