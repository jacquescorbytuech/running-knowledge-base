# Elliott-Sale et al. 2020, oral contraceptives and performance meta-analysis

Systematic review and meta-analysis of oral contraceptives and exercise performance. The pooled effect was very close to zero, so oral contraceptives are unlikely to have any meaningful average effect on performance, though individual responses vary and study quality is mixed. Moderate as a meta-analysis of low-quality primary studies.
