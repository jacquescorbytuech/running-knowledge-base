# Lastella et al. 2021, To nap or not to nap, napping in athletes

Systematic review of napping in athletes; favourable effects on physical and cognitive performance, alertness and mood, largest in sleep-restricted athletes; recommends 20-90 min naps between 13:00 and 16:00 with ~30 min after to clear inertia. Moderate.
