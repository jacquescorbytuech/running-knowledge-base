# Blanchfield et al. 2023, Endurance sport coaching in an online space

Qualitative interview study of seven endurance-sport coaches working online. Remote coaches use messaging and software to maintain a coaching presence and deliberately foster athlete autonomy and self-regulation out of necessity, while flagging accountability and adherence risks when supervision is asynchronous. A small qualitative sample. Limited.
