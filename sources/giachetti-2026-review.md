# Giachetti Martin et al. 2026, Carbon plates in running shoes: systematic review and meta-analysis

16 randomised crossover studies: borderline reduction in ankle power (authors flag potential relevance for Achilles tendinopathy and tibial/patellofemoral loading); no other consistent joint-level biomechanical change; no direct injury evidence either way. Moderate.
