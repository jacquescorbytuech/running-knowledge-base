# Medbo et al. 1988, accumulated oxygen deficit

Introduced the maximal accumulated oxygen deficit (MAOD) as a measure of anaerobic capacity, derived from the gap between the oxygen demand of supramaximal exercise and the oxygen actually taken up. The founding validation study for MAOD. Controlled laboratory work.
