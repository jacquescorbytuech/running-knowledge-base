# El Helou et al. 2012, Environmental parameters and marathon performance

Analysis of roughly 1.8 million marathon performances across six major races. Air temperature was the environmental factor with the greatest impact on finishing times, and performance was optimised in cool conditions, declining as temperature rose above a low optimum and also at the cold end. Strong.
