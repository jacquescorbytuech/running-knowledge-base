# Beliard et al. 2015, Compression garments and exercise, no effect of pressure

Review finding that the effects of compression garments bear no clear relation to the pressure applied, since benefits appeared at both low and high pressures. This undercuts the venous-return mechanism and points toward comfort, proprioception or placebo as the driver. Moderate (against a pressure-driven mechanism).
