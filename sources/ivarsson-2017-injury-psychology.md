# Ivarsson et al. 2017, Psychosocial factors and sport injury

Systematic review and meta-analysis. Negative life-event stress was the psychosocial factor most consistently associated with higher injury rates in athletes; daily hassles and prior injury also raised risk. Stress-management interventions reduced injury occurrence. Meta-analysis of mostly team-sport cohorts. Moderate.
