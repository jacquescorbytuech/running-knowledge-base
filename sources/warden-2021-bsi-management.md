# Warden et al. 2021, Managing low-risk tibial and metatarsal bone stress injuries

Clinical commentary on optimal loading for low-risk bone stress injuries in runners. Low-risk sites tolerate a graded, symptom-guided return where pain stays at or near 0/10 during, after and the next day; high-risk sites need offloading and closer supervision. Moderate (expert synthesis of the load-management literature).
