# 2024 meta-analysis, self-reported disordered eating in athletes

Systematic review, meta-analysis and meta-regression of 177 studies and 70,957 athletes. Mean prevalence of self-reported disordered eating was about 19%, with female sex and lean sports among the risk factors. Moderate; large but self-report based.
