# Tanaka, Monahan & Seals 2001, age-predicted maximal heart rate

Meta-analysis establishing that maximal heart rate falls with age at roughly 0.7 beats per minute per year, independent of sex and habitual activity (equation 208 minus 0.7 times age). The standard reference for age-related decline in maximal heart rate. A meta-analysis.
