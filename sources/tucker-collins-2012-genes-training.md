# Tucker and Collins 2012, What makes champions? Genes and training in sporting success

Review in *British Journal of Sports Medicine* 46(8):555–561. Elite performance is the interaction of genetic ceiling and training: training realises genetic potential but cannot by itself produce an elite athlete. Supports the claim that elites are physiological outliers, not ordinary runners with more miles.
