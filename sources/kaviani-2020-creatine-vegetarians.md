# Kaviani et al. 2020, Creatine supplementation in vegetarians, a review

Systematic review of creatine supplementation in vegetarians. Vegetarians and vegans have lower resting muscle creatine (roughly 20 to 30% lower) and therefore tend to show a larger response to supplementation than omnivores, with greater gains toward the saturation ceiling. Consistent across the included trials. Moderate to strong.
