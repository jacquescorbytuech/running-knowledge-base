# Sandford et al. 2019, anaerobic speed reserve in 800m runners

Anaerobic speed reserve, the gap between velocity at VO2max and maximal sprint speed, distinguished the world's best 800m runners into distinct speed-based and endurance-based profiles. Argues maximal sprint speed and ASR are under-used tools for middle-distance profiling. An observational study of elite athletes.
