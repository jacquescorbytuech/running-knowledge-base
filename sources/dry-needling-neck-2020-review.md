# Dry needling for trigger-point neck pain 2020 (updated systematic review)

Updated systematic review and meta-analysis of dry needling for myofascial trigger points associated with neck pain. Low-to-moderate-quality evidence supported a short-term reduction in pain and improvement in range of motion versus sham or no treatment, but the medium- and long-term effects were not established, and trial quality was a recurring limitation. Consistent with the wider dry-needling literature across conditions: short-term pain relief at best, on thin evidence, with no durable advantage.
