# Engel et al. 2016, Can runners benefit from compression clothing

Review of compression clothing in running. No statistically significant mean effect on running performance across distances, with only small effects on running economy and biomechanical variables; the more consistent benefits were post-exercise, on perceived leg soreness and delayed muscle fatigue. Moderate.
