# Hoogkamer et al. 2018, Metabolic cost of running in prototype racing shoes

Prototype Vaporfly reduced metabolic cost ~4% vs racing flats, mass equalised; all 18 runners improved, range -1.6% to -6.3%. Nike-funded, independent lab. Strong.
