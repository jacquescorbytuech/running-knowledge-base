# Henriksen et al. 2010, Holistic ecological approach to talent development

Case study introducing the athletic talent development environment (ATDE) framework: athlete development is best understood as the interaction between an athlete and their whole environment, not the individual in isolation. Successful environments combine clear long-term goals, coherent support and a strong club culture. Conceptually influential but built on qualitative case studies rather than controlled trials. Moderate.
