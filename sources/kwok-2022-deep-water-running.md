# Kwok et al. 2022, Deep water running: cardiorespiratory fitness systematic review

A systematic review of six databases identified 11 clinical trials (287 participants) examining the effectiveness of deep water running on cardiorespiratory fitness, physical function and quality of life. Results showed that deep water running produces cardiorespiratory effects comparable to land-based training across diverse populations and comparison conditions. Evidence is moderate.
