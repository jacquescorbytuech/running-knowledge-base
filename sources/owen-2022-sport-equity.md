# Owen et al. 2022, Participation equity in organised sport, a meta-analysis

Systematic review and meta-analysis of participation equity in organised sport and physical activity among children and adolescents in high-income countries. Those from higher-socioeconomic-status households were roughly 1.87 times more likely to take part in sport, and the gap was larger for organised sport than for total physical activity. Strong.
