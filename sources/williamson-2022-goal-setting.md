# Williamson et al. 2022, Goal setting in sport, a meta-analysis

Systematic review and meta-analysis of goal setting in sport. Goal setting produces small but reliable performance and psychological benefits, with specific, moderately difficult goals outperforming vague "do your best" or excessively hard goals. Effects are modest and variable, and much of the older evidence used non-athlete samples. Moderate.
