# Lane et al. 2019, low energy availability in male endurance athletes

Survey of competitively trained male endurance athletes finding a high proportion at risk of low energy availability, with running among the affected sports. Demonstrates that men are not exempt from the root cause of RED-S. Limited to moderate; self-report screening in one cohort.
