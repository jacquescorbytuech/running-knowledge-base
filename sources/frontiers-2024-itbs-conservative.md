# Frontiers 2024, Conservative treatment for iliotibial band syndrome in runners

Systematic review of conservative management of iliotibial band syndrome in runners. Hip-abductor strengthening reduced pain and improved function across studies; load management and relative rest are the foundation, while passive measures alone are weakly supported. Moderate (small heterogeneous studies).
