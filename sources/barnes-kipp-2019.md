# Barnes & Kilding 2019, Independent validation of the retail Vaporfly

Retail Vaporfly improved running economy by 2.6% versus track spikes and 4.2% versus a marketed marathon racing shoe (2.9% when weight-matched), with wide individual variation; the headline 4% figure is comparison-shoe-dependent. Strong.
