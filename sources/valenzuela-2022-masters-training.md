# Valenzuela et al. 2022, training and cardiorespiratory loss in masters

Review of cardiorespiratory decline in ageing endurance athletes: longitudinal VO2max losses range widely with training, and around half the variance in decline is explained by changes in training volume; athletes who hold training lose roughly half as fast as sedentary peers. A review of longitudinal data.
