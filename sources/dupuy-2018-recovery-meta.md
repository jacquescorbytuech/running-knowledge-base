# Dupuy et al. 2018, Recovery techniques meta-analysis

Massage, cold water immersion and several other modalities reduced delayed-onset muscle soreness, with massage the most effective for both soreness and perceived fatigue; active recovery reduced soreness but not perceived fatigue. Effects are largely symptomatic. Moderate.
