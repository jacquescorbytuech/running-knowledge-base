# Athlete's heart, physiological remodelling (Pluim et al. review)

Endurance training causes eccentric left-ventricular remodelling: larger chamber size with proportional wall thickening. This is a benign adaptation that regresses with detraining, which is the key feature distinguishing it from pathological hypertrophy such as cardiomyopathy. Review of echocardiographic data. Moderate.
