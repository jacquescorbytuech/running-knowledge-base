# Shockwave therapy for patellar, Achilles tendinopathy and plantar fasciitis 2023 (meta-analysis)

Systematic review and meta-analysis of extracorporeal shockwave therapy across three load-related lower-limb conditions: patellar tendinopathy, Achilles tendinopathy and plantar fasciitis. ESWT reduced pain compared with control across these chronic conditions, supporting its use as an adjunct, particularly for cases that have not responded to first-line loading. Effects are condition-specific: the evidence is strongest for chronic, recalcitrant plantar fasciitis and Achilles tendinopathy rather than for soft-tissue complaints in general.
