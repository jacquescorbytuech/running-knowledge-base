# Broatch et al. 2019, Compression reduces muscle movement during running

Study of compression tights during submaximal running. The tights reduced thigh muscle displacement, soft-tissue vibration and muscle activation across a range of speeds, yet had no measurable effect on running economy. A real, measurable mechanism that does not translate into a performance benefit. Moderate.
