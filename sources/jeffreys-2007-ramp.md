# Jeffreys 2007, the RAMP method of warm-up

Practitioner article (Professional Strength and Conditioning) proposing the RAMP structure for warm-ups: Raise temperature and blood flow, Activate key muscles and Mobilise joints, then Potentiate with high-intensity activity specific to the session. A coaching framework rather than a trial, widely adopted in strength and conditioning.
