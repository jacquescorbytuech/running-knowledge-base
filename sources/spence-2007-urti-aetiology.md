# Spence et al. 2007, Aetiology of upper respiratory illness in athletes

Study identifying the cause of upper-respiratory illness episodes in elite and recreational athletes. In a large share of symptomatic episodes no infectious pathogen was found, so many "colds" in athletes are not confirmed infections and may reflect allergy, asthma or airway irritation. Undercuts symptom-based infection counts. Moderate.
