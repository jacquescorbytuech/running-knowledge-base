# Hernández-Lougedo et al. 2023, Vegetarian diet and sports performance, a review

Systematic review of vegetarian diets and sports performance. Vegetarian and vegan athletes typically eat more carbohydrate and less protein than omnivores, but measured performance markers such as VO₂max, power and sprint do not differ significantly. No performance edge from the diet itself. Moderate.
