# Schwellnus et al. 2012, Travel and illness in athletes

Study of an international rugby tournament. Athletes travelling across more than five time zones had a two-to-three-fold higher risk of illness than those at home or travelling shorter distances, with respiratory infections most common. Combines cabin exposure, circadian disruption and training-related immune suppression. Observational cohort. Moderate.
