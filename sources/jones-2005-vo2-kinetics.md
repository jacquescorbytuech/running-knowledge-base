# Poole & Jones 2012, oxygen uptake kinetics (review)

Review of VO2 kinetics: at the onset of hard exercise oxygen uptake rises with a delay, and the size of the early oxygen deficit, plus the slow component above the lactate threshold, reflect how much energy must come anaerobically. Faster kinetics from training reduce reliance on anaerobic stores. A review of controlled physiology.
