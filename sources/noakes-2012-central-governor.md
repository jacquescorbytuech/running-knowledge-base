# Noakes 2012, fatigue as a brain-derived emotion

Statement of the central governor model: the brain regulates exercise intensity in anticipation, via afferent feedback and conscious effort, to protect homeostasis, so motor recruitment is held in reserve and exhaustion is reached before true muscle failure. The leading articulation of the central-regulation view, and a contested one. A review and position paper.
