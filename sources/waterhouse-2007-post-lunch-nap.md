# Waterhouse et al. 2007, Short post-lunch nap after partial sleep deprivation

After a night cut to four hours, a 30-minute post-lunch nap improved alertness, short-term memory, reaction-time accuracy and sprint times (2 m and 20 m). Small crossover study. Moderate.
