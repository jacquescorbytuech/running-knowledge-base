# Validity of Cooper's 12-minute run test for estimating VO2max

Field-test estimation of VO2max from a 12-minute maximal run; predicted values differed significantly from directly measured VO2max in the study sample, and accuracy varies by population and pacing skill. A practical screening estimate, not a substitute for laboratory measurement. Moderate/limited.
