# Reliability and validity of the Åstrand nomogram for submaximal VO2max estimation

Submaximal cycle-test estimation of VO2max correlates with measured values around r = 0.85-0.90 but with a standard error of estimate of roughly 5-6 ml/kg/min, the cost of estimating a maximum from a submaximal heart-rate response. Moderate.
