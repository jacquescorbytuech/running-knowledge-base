# England Athletics, Benefits of club affiliation

England Athletics' statement of what club affiliation provides: eligibility to enter most licensed competitions (for registered athletes of affiliated clubs), public liability insurance cover, and the ability to apply for Track & Field and Cross Country competition licences. Affiliation and individual athlete registration carry annual fees set each year. Strong as an organisational fact; fee figures are time-bound.
