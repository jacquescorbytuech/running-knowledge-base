# Flint v. Strava, segment-chasing and risk

Reporting on the wrongful-death suit that followed a cyclist's fatal crash while attempting to reclaim a Strava segment record (KOM). The court dismissed the claim on assumption-of-risk grounds. A single legal case, not epidemiological evidence, but a documented instance of leaderboard competition driving real-world risk-taking. Limited.
