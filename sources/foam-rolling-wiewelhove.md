# Wiewelhove et al. 2019, Foam rolling and performance/recovery (meta-analysis)

Meta-analysis: foam rolling produces small, short-lived increases in range of motion and small reductions in perceived muscle soreness, with negligible effects on muscle function. Pre-rolling may slightly aid sprint performance and flexibility; post-rolling slightly reduces perceived soreness. Effects are minor and largely perceptual. Limited.
