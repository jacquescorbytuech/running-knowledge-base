# Simpson et al. 2020, Can exercise increase infection susceptibility, consensus

Consensus statement from the leading exercise-immunology groups. Infection susceptibility in athletes is multifactorial, and whether exercise itself causally increases infection risk, independent of travel, crowding, sleep loss, life stress and low energy availability, remains unresolved. A deliberately cautious reconciliation of the J-curve and its critics. Strong (consensus).
