# Nascimento et al. 2018, Hip and knee strengthening for patellofemoral pain

Systematic review with meta-analysis (14 trials, 673 participants): adding hip-focused strengthening to knee strengthening beats knee strengthening alone for reducing pain and improving activity in patellofemoral pain. Strong (pooled RCTs).
