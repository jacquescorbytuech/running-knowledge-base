# Stenfors et al. 2022, Breathing mask attenuates airway response in sub-zero air

Controlled study in healthy subjects. A heat-and-moisture-exchanging breathing mask attenuated the acute airway response to exercise in sub-zero air, supporting covering the mouth and nose (a buff or such a mask) to reduce airway heat and water loss in very cold conditions. Moderate (small controlled study).
