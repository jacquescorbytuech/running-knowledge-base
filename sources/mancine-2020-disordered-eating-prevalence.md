# Mancine et al. 2020, disordered eating by sport leanness, systematic review

Systematic review of disordered-eating prevalence in athletes grouped by emphasis on leanness and by activity type. Disordered eating is most common in sports that stress leanness, aesthetics, weight class or endurance, including distance running, and higher than in non-lean sports and non-athletes. Moderate; heterogeneous self-report screening tools.
