# Elliott-Sale et al. 2021, methodological considerations for female research

Methodological guidance on studying female athletes, noting that most menstrual-cycle performance studies fail to verify cycle phase with hormone measurement, which inflates and confuses reported effects. Supports individualising by tracked symptoms rather than prescribing by phase. Moderate as a methods consensus.
