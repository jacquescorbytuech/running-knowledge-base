# Besson et al. 2022, sex differences in endurance running

Review of sex differences in endurance running. Documents the stable ~10–12% sex performance gap, women's higher relative fat oxidation and greater fatigue resistance in some tasks, and the under-representation of women in sports-science research. Moderate as a narrative review.
