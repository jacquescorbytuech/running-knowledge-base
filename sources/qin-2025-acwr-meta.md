# Qin et al. 2025, ACWR and sports injury risk: systematic review and meta-analysis

This meta-analysis of 22 single-arm cohort studies found a statistically significant positive association between high acute:chronic workload ratio (ACWR) values and injury occurrence (effect size 0.72), with the 0.8–1.3 range associated with the lowest injury risk across the included studies. The authors conclude that while elevated ACWR (above approximately 1.3) carries meaningful predictive signal, the ratio's utility is constrained by the methodological limitations identified in the wider literature. Evidence is moderate.
