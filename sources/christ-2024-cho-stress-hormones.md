# Christ et al. 2024, carbohydrate and immunoregulatory stress hormones (systematic review)

Systematic review of 11 studies in experienced endurance athletes. Pre- and mid-exercise carbohydrate ingestion attenuated the cortisol rise (large effect) and, more modestly, epinephrine; norepinephrine was largely unaffected. Frontiers in Sports and Active Living. Moderate.
