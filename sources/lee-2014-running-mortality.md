# Lee et al. 2014, Leisure-time running and mortality

Aerobics Center Longitudinal Study cohort (55,137 adults, 15-year follow-up). Runners had 30% lower all-cause and 45% lower cardiovascular mortality than non-runners, and lived about three years longer. Benefits were similar across running doses, including low volumes (under 51 min/week). Observational. Moderate.
