# Almeida, Davis & Lopes 2015, foot-strike biomechanics

Systematic review with meta-analysis (16 studies) of biomechanical differences between foot-strike patterns. Rearfoot strikers showed higher vertical loading rates; forefoot strikers landed with a plantar-flexed ankle and more flexed knee. No pattern was identified as clearly superior for performance or injury. A review of mixed-quality evidence.
