# Martín-Rincón & Calbet 2020, Progress update and challenges on VO2max testing and interpretation

Frontiers in Physiology review of maximal oxygen uptake testing: the secondary criteria (VO2 plateau defined as a rise under ~100 ml/min despite increasing work, RER thresholds, heart rate near age-predicted max, RPE), their limitations, and the verification-phase approach to confirming a true maximum. Strong.
