# Poppendieck et al. 2016, Massage and performance recovery (meta-analysis)

Sports Medicine meta-analysis of manual massage for recovery: small positive effects on perceived recovery and soreness, with only minor and inconsistent effects on the recovery of physical performance. The benefits are largest for perception and relaxation rather than measured function. Limited, mostly perceptual.
