# Gastin & Suppiah 2026, Anaerobic and aerobic energy system contribution during maximal exercise: systematic review

A systematic review of 102 studies with 311 data points found that the crossover point at which aerobic and anaerobic contributions are equal occurs at 78.6 s of maximal exercise (95% CI ±1.1 s); beyond that duration the aerobic system supplies progressively more energy. Pooled estimates for events lasting roughly 2 minutes (800 m) place the aerobic share at approximately 66%, rising substantially by 1500 m duration, consistent with earlier single-cohort data but now synthesised across three methodological approaches. Strong evidence.
