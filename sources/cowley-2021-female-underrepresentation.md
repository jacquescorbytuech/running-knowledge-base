# Cowley et al. 2021, women in sport and exercise science research

Analysis of sport and exercise science publications finding that women make up a small minority of participants, with many studies male-only. Quantifies the male-default problem that limits how well findings transfer to female athletes. Moderate; bibliometric analysis.
