# Mihály Iglói (biographical overview)

Hungarian distance coach (1908-1998) who coached Sándor Iharos, István Rózsavölgyi, László Tábori, Bob Schul and Jim Beatty. His athletes set 49 world records across recognised and unofficial distances. Biographical reference, moderate reliability.
