# Mountjoy et al. 2014, Beyond the female athlete triad, RED-S

The original IOC consensus statement that introduced Relative Energy Deficiency in Sport (RED-S). It broadened the older female athlete triad of low energy availability, menstrual dysfunction and low bone density into a wider syndrome affecting many body systems and both sexes, with low energy availability as the shared cause. Strong as a consensus statement.
