# Boullosa et al. 2020, Factors affecting training in recreational endurance runners

Review of training and performance factors in recreational endurance runners. Recreational runners frequently misjudge their intensity distribution, spending too much time at moderate intensity, and the authors recommend a predominantly low-intensity (polarised or pyramidal) model. Structuring intensity is one concrete thing a coach or a disciplined self-coached runner can correct. Moderate.
