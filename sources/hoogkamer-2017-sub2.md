# Hoogkamer, Kram & Arellano 2017, Modeling a sub-2-hour marathon

Models that a 2.7% reduction in metabolic cost is sufficient to break the two-hour marathon barrier; identifies biomechanical strategies — lighter shoes (~100 g), strategic drafting with alternating lead runners, a tailwind, and a slight elevation drop — that could collectively achieve this threshold. Strong (modelling).
