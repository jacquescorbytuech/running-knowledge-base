# Burnley, Bearden & Jones 2022, Polarised training point-counterpoint

Med Sci Sports Exerc exchange: polarised training is not proven optimal. The paper's primary arguments are that what is labelled 'polarised' is often pyramidal when training is measured by time rather than session count, and that elite athletes rarely train in a truly polarised distribution. Contested.
