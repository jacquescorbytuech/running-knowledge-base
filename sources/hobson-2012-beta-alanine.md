# Hobson et al. 2012, Beta-alanine supplementation and exercise meta-analysis

Benefit concentrated in 1-4 min maximal efforts (~2.85%), significant 60-240 s but not below 60 s; a smaller but still significant benefit was also found for efforts exceeding 240 s (P=0.046). Strong.
