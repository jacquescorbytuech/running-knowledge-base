# Quirk et al. 2023, parkrun, mental health and social inclusion

Analysis of parkrun participants with mental-health conditions, drawn from the parkrun UK Health and Wellbeing Survey. Respondents reported benefits to mental health and a sense of social inclusion, and volunteering was associated with particularly strong perceived benefits for this group. Cross-sectional and self-reported; it describes participants' experiences rather than a controlled treatment effect. Limited.
