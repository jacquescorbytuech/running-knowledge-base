# Menzies et al. 2010, Active recovery and lactate clearance

Study of blood lactate clearance after an intense running bout. Active recovery cleared lactate faster than passive rest, and clearance was fastest at an intensity around the lactate threshold. Confirms the clearance mechanism, but says nothing about whether faster clearance improves later performance. Small controlled study. Moderate for the mechanism.
