# Whittaker et al. 2018, Foot orthoses for plantar heel pain, a systematic review and meta-analysis

Systematic review and meta-analysis (Whittaker GA, Munteanu SE, Menz HB, Tan JM, Rabusin CL, Landorf KB), *British Journal of Sports Medicine*. Foot orthoses give modest pain relief in plantar heel pain in the medium term (about 7 to 12 weeks), of uncertain clinical importance, with little effect on function, and prefabricated and customised devices perform similarly. Moderate-quality evidence; moderate.
