# Hoenig et al. 2022, MRI grading and return to sport after bone stress injuries

Systematic review and meta-analysis of MRI grading (Fredericson and similar) against return-to-sport time after bone stress injuries. Higher MRI grade is associated with longer time to return, though the relationship is imperfect and site matters more for risk of complication. Moderate (pooled observational data).
