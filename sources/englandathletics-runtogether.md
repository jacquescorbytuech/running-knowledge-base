# England Athletics, RunTogether programme

England Athletics' RunTogether scheme organises ability-graded community group runs, from beginner to advanced, across England, with online tools to find a local group. The participation figures quoted on the site are organisational self-report rather than audited research, so treat the counts as indicative. Limited (organisational source).
