# Parreira et al. 2014, Current evidence does not support Kinesio Taping (systematic review)

Journal of Physiotherapy systematic review of randomised trials of Kinesio Taping for musculoskeletal conditions. Across the trials, taping was no better than sham taping, placebo or active comparators; where any advantage appeared, it was too small to be clinically worthwhile or came from low-quality trials. The authors concluded that current evidence does not support the use of Kinesio Taping in clinical practice. A relevant part of any felt benefit is the tactile sensation of the tape, a placebo route.
