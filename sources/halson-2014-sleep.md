# Halson 2014, Sleep in elite athletes and nutritional interventions to enhance sleep

Reviews sleep's importance for athletes and nutritional aids to improve it; notes limited direct evidence on sleep's role in recovery, with the 9-10 hour athlete recommendation leaning on extension studies. Limited-moderate.
