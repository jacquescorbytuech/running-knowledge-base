# Knopp et al. 2023, Variability in response to footwear

Sports Med: individual super-shoe responses varied by cohort — world-class Kenyan runners ranged from 11.3% drawback to 11.4% benefit, while amateur Europeans ranged from 1.1% drawback to 9.7% benefit; world-class runners showed no significant mean benefit. Strong. Strong.
