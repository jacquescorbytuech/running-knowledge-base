# Craddock et al. 2016, Vegetarian versus omnivorous physical performance

Systematic review of eight studies comparing physical performance in vegetarian and omnivorous athletes. No difference was found in muscular strength, power, or aerobic or anaerobic performance, supporting that a vegetarian diet neither impairs nor enhances performance per se when nutrition is adequate. The underlying trials are few, small and heterogeneous. Moderate.
