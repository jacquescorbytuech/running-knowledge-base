# Niklewicz et al. 2023, Vitamin B12 and plant-based diets

Review of vitamin B12 for people on plant-based diets. B12 is effectively absent from unfortified plant foods because plants do not synthesise it, so reliable supplementation or fortified foods are essential for vegans; deficiency causes anaemia and fatigue that directly degrade endurance capacity. Strong (uncontested physiology).
