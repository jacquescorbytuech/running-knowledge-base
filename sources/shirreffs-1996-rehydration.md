# Shirreffs et al. 1996, post-exercise rehydration: volume and drink sodium

To restore fluid balance after exercise a drink volume greater than sweat loss must be ingested; unless the drink's sodium content is high enough, the surplus is simply lost as urine. Tested 50, 100, 150 and 200% of mass loss at differing sodium concentrations. Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise. Strong.
