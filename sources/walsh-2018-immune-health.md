# Walsh 2018, Recommendations to maintain immune health in athletes

Review of practical steps to maintain immune health in athletes. The best-supported levers are basic and non-pharmacological: hand hygiene, avoiding sick contacts, adequate sleep, sensible load progression to avoid overreaching, and adequate energy and carbohydrate availability. Also describes the open-window concept that later work has reframed. Moderate (consensus-backed bundle).
