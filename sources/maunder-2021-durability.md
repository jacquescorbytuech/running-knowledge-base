# Maunder et al. 2021, The Importance of 'Durability' in the Physiological Profiling of Endurance Athletes

Sports Medicine review proposing durability, the resistance to deterioration of physiological-profile characteristics during prolonged exercise, as a fourth determinant of endurance performance alongside VO2max, threshold and economy. Conceptual review, widely accepted; moderate.
