# Extracorporeal shockwave therapy for Achilles tendinopathy 2022 (systematic review)

Sports Medicine - Open systematic review of randomised controlled trials of extracorporeal shockwave therapy (ESWT) for mid-portion and insertional Achilles tendinopathy. Moderate-quality evidence supported a benefit of ESWT over standard care, with a pooled improvement on the VISA-A score of about 9 points, and the best results came from combining ESWT with eccentric loading rather than using it alone. One of the better-evidenced passive adjuncts for a specific tendinopathy, though still an adjunct to loading.
