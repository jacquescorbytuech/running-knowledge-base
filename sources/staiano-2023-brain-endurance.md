# Staiano et al. 2023, Brain endurance training in cyclists

Trial of brain endurance training (BET), which pairs demanding cognitive tasks with physical training to build tolerance to mental fatigue. Combined cognitive-plus-physical training improved endurance and cognitive performance more than matched physical training alone. Promising but built on small samples, mostly in cycling, with little competition transfer shown. Limited.
