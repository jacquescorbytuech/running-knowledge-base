# Tenforde et al. 2024, High- and low-risk bone stress injury in female runners

Study of female runners stratifying bone stress injuries by site risk. Trabecular-rich, high-risk sites cluster with markers of low energy availability and impaired bone health (low BMD, menstrual dysfunction, restrictive eating), supporting risk-factor screening. Moderate.
