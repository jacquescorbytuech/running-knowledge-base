# PFP consensus and reviews 2016-2018, Exercise therapy for patellofemoral pain

Systematic review with meta-analysis and the Manchester/Gold Coast consensus statements place combined hip and knee strengthening at the top of the evidence for patellofemoral pain. Adding hip-focused work beats knee strengthening alone for pain and function. Strong (consensus plus pooled RCTs).
