# Llanos-Lagos et al. 2024, Resistance training and running economy across speeds

Heavy-load (>80% 1RM), plyometric, and combined resistance training each improve running economy, but only at specific speed ranges. Submaximal (40–79% 1RM) and isometric training showed no improvement. Moderate.
