# Owens et al. 2018, vitamin D and the athlete

Review of vitamin D in athletes. Argues against routine megadosing, noting that very high bolus doses drive vitamin D catabolism and may be counterproductive, and supports correcting a measured deficiency toward a sufficiency target rather than chasing high concentrations. Roles in bone are clear; effects on muscle function and performance are less certain. Moderate as an expert review.
